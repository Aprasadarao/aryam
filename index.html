Welcome to

Aryam Health Care

Aryam Health Care is technology and traditionally driven quality care with empathy and within reach at affordable cost to deliver protocol driven, evidence based interventions from highly qualified medical professionals with updated skills in early recovery from ailments.
We love to provide standardized health care services at home with responsibility and we are available for you.

“With empathy ..”, as a driving element in Home Health Care, Dr Sujeer Basha an alumnus of Indian School of Business (AMPH) and from the first batch stream of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas has incorporated Aryam Health Care, from Hyderabad. Apart from leading the physio vertical for Apollo Homecare for over 5 years, he has also worked as Asst Professor in Deccan College of Medical Sciences College of Physiotherapy and was in charge of the Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation Department for many years in Owaisi Hospital and Research Centre. He brings his telemedicine work experience of 104 services to the palette. With Aryam Health Care he integrates his more than decade years of experience with teams of dedicated health front liners to promote quality home health care within the comfort and convenience of the patient’s home with Quality, Availability and Affordability.
